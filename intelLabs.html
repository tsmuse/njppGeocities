Intel Labs

Date Completed: 2012

Project Type: Autoomotive computing

Solved: Feature strategy, Information architecture, Interaction design, Usability testing

Live at: not published

The Project

Intel brought me in for a short assignment to help them with an innovative automotive product. I acted as the lead interaction designer and worked on sense-making and feature development tasks as well as producing fully fleshed out narratives and wireframes to aid the development team in building a prototype for extensive user testing. The project focused on solving many common problems in ubiquitous systems such as context of use, ease of use while distracted, and distraction due to the technology. Due to NDA restrictions from Intel, I cannot show any of details of the work I did online. I can show the work to prospective employers in-person or via screen sharing.
